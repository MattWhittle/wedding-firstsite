Our Story



Danielle DeVizia & Matt Whittle met on
July 31st, 2008.

Danielle & Holly were roomies
& Chi Omega sisters.
Holly always said,
“If you ever met my brother,
you guys would probably
fall in love.”

And then they met.
And they did.



They met halfway
in Virginia Beach
and shared a sunrise,
a slice of pizza,
and a day on the beach.



Danielle moved
to Philly,
and they spent four years
of Zoo dates,
Steve’s cheesesteaks,
and nights walking
under the
Ben Franklin Bridge.

66 Eagles Games 4 Erin Expresses
24 Dates to the Philadelphia Zoo 2 Graduations
6 Best Dad Shows 1 Epic 65 Mile Bike Ride




Matt and Danielle moved to Charlotte
in May of 2013
and brought little
120 pound Hamlet
into their lives.

In October of 2014,
Matt and Danielle went to Philly
to see Jeff and Talia
get married.

Matt & AJ convinced
Danielle to go out to
“one bar”
for “one drink”
after the wedding.
It was 40 degrees
that night
and Danielle was
in a dress.



When they got out of the cab and there was no bar around, only the Ben Franklin Bridge, Danielle was cold and confused. AJ offered to take a picture of them, and after some stalling, Danielle saw their friends, and her best friend Kat, run out to meet them. Danielle turned around, and Matt was down on one knee.





Matt and Danielle
begin the next chapter
of their lives
on July 10th, 2016.
